Beats Pill Consumer Sentiment Analysis

During my externship as a Data Analyst at Beats By Dre, I conducted in-depth analyses of competitors' products and consumer feedback, identifying strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.

I utilized advanced natural language processing tools, including Gemini AI, to interpret and summarize customer feedback, with a focus on sentiment analysis to inform product development. My work provided actionable insights to the Consumer Insights Team, leading to enhanced product strategies. I identified three key areas of improvement for the new Beats Pill, contributing to a projected 5% increase in customer satisfaction. Additionally, I presented findings and recommendations to the Consumer insights team, influencing decision-making processes and supporting business objectives.
