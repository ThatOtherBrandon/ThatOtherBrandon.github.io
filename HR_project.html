HR Analytics: Absenteeism

Project Background

This project was given to me by the HR department, aiming to reward employees for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. My tasks included identifying eligible employees for a health bonus program, calculating annual compensation adjustments for non-smokers, and developing a dashboard to give the HR team better insights into workplace absenteeism and employee health metrics.


Solution Approach

  • Analyzed employee health data to filter those meeting criteria for the healthy bonus program
  • Calculated annual compensation adjustments, targeting non-smokers for wage increases totaling $1,414.40 per year per non-smoker, within an insurance budget of $983,221.
  • Developed a dynamic dashboard based on an approved wireframe to visualize absenteeism trends and provide actionable insights for the HR department.

Key Insights and Impact

The project enabled the HR department to identify and reward health-conscious employees effectively, providing clarity on absenteeism patterns. The dashboard offered an accessible, real-time view of absenteeism, aiding in more informed HR decisions.
